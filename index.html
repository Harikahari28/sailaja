About Plantation Awareness

Plantation awareness is crucial for the sustainability of our environment. Planting trees helps to combat climate change, improve air quality, and provide habitat for wildlife. This program aims to educate and encourage people to participate in tree plantation activities.

Importance of Plantations

  • Reduce carbon footprint
  • Improve air and water quality
  • Provide shelter and food for wildlife
  • Enhance the beauty of landscapes

Organic Pesticides

Use organic options like neem oil, insecticidal soaps, and horticultural oils to manage pests. These are less harmful to beneficial insects and the environment.

Regular Inspections

Frequently inspect plants for signs of pests or diseases. Early detection can help manage problems before they spread.

Remove Infected Plants

Promptly remove and destroy any infected plants to prevent the spread of diseases.

Plant Protection Tips

Protecting young plants from pests and diseases is crucial for their healthy growth and development. Here are some effective practices:

Clean Tools

Use clean tools and equipment to prevent the spread of pathogens.

Healthy Soil

Maintain healthy soil through proper fertilization and organic matter to promote strong plant growth.

Watering Practices

Water at the base of plants. Water in the morning so plants can dry during the day.

Fencing

Protect plants from birds and animals using fences or nets.
